about

'Bathe in the Light of the Miracle Lounge:

Donor Lens have designed a set of slippery varispeed jams and airport ambient works to soundtrack your visit.

They have used only the finest fretless instruments and vintage synthesizers. Members are free to adjust the playback speed to taste.

In the Lounge, you can dream of your destination; deals done; or simply enjoy The Moment

credits

released October 12, 2019

Donors Lens plays Roland SH-101, Juno 106, Yamaha DX100 and Dave Smith Prophet synths; fretless guitar, bass and sitar. Occasionally, they sing. Guest vocals come in Russian and Cantonese from Alex Nikiporenko and Coco Wong. Miracle narration by Jana Tyrrell.

Mixed by Donor Lens
Mastered by Love in Dust
Artwork by Victor Arce (@lxtxcx)

Donor Lens is Thom Hosken (Love in Dust) & Jerome 'Jay' Sabourin (Kid Neon)
